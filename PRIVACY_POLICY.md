# Privacy Policy for NicFree

_Last updated: May 9, 2026_

This Privacy Policy describes how the NicFree mobile application ("NicFree", "we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

By installing or using NicFree you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide
NicFree is designed to work without an account. The following information is **stored locally on your device** and is not transmitted to our servers:

- Your first name (optional, used for greetings inside the App)
- Your quit date and quit reason
- Your previous nicotine usage details (product type, daily amount, cost) used to calculate savings and health milestones
- Logged cravings, slips, breath sessions, journal entries, and other progress data you create inside the App

### 1.2 Subscription information
If you purchase a NicFree Pro subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple via the Apple App Store. We use **RevenueCat** to manage subscription state and verify entitlements. The following information may be processed:

- An anonymous user ID generated by RevenueCat
- Apple's subscription receipt and entitlement data
- Purchase events (start, renewal, cancellation, refund)

We do **not** receive your full name, email, billing address, or payment details from Apple. Payment information is handled exclusively by Apple.

### 1.3 Information collected automatically
The App does not contain third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not collect device location, contacts, photos, or microphone data.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information solely to operate the App's features:

- Display your personalized progress, savings, and milestones inside the App
- Activate and verify your Pro subscription (via RevenueCat and Apple)
- Restore previous purchases when you sign in with the same Apple ID

## 3. How We Share Information

We do **not** sell your information. Limited information is shared with the following service providers strictly to provide the subscription functionality:

- **Apple Inc.** — processes payments and manages your subscription. See https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **RevenueCat, Inc.** — manages subscription state. See https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your personal progress data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS app storage. If you delete the App, your local data is removed.

Subscription-state data held by Apple and RevenueCat is retained according to their respective policies and applicable law.

## 5. Children's Privacy

NicFree is intended for users **17 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

## 6. Your Rights

Because most of your data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App.

If you would like to request deletion of subscription-state data associated with your Apple ID, contact us using the address below and we will request deletion from RevenueCat on your behalf.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was most recently revised. Material changes will be communicated within the App or by updating this page.
